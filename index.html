About Me

My name is Nyashadzashe Gumboguru, a 24-year-old tech enthusiast, CEO of Access Soft Labs, data analyst, and web developer. I’m currently pursuing my degree in Software Engineering.

I have a deep passion for technology and a love for simplicity, reflected in my preference for black and white. Faith is central to my life I value prayer and respect God. As the eldest of three boys, I embrace leadership and responsibility.

I reside in Kwekwe, Msasa Eastview, Aspedel, Zimbabwe. I’m excited to be learning and growing with a great family here at BYU–Idaho .

My company, Access Soft Labs, is just starting out, and I’m committed to building it into something impactful by combining innovation, dedication, and service to others.
