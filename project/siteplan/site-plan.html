Murudo Site Plan

A global dating platform that connects hearts across borders.

Site Name

Murudo – “Murudo” means love in Shona. The name is unique, memorable, and easy to brand internationally.

Optional domain: murudo-dating.com

Site Purpose

The purpose of Murudo is to help people form genuine romantic connections across cultures. The site will provide:

  • User profiles with photos and bios.
  • A blog section where users can share love stories.
  • A simple sign-up form for new users.

Scenarios

  • “How do I sign up to meet other people on Murudo?”
  • “Can I read love stories and confessions shared by other couples?”
  • “Is Murudo available for people outside Zimbabwe?”

Color Schema

Primary Color: #b30059 (deep rose) – used for headings, accents, buttons, and the header background.

Secondary Color: #f9f9f9 (light neutral) – used for section backgrounds, cards, and contrast against dark text.

Supporting text color: #333333 (dark gray) – for paragraphs and body text.

Typography

Headings: Playfair Display (serif) – gives an elegant, romantic feel.

Body Text: Roboto (sans-serif) – clean, modern, and easy to read.
